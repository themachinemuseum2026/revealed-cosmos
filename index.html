About

I turn unfinished concepts into production-ready story material.

I design narrative systems and worldbuilding architecture for games, RPGs, interactive media, and long-form fictional IPs. My work helps creators turn scattered ideas into clear material that collaborators can read, discuss, revise, and build from.

Many projects begin with strong ideas but scattered structure: partial lore, disconnected mechanics, unclear character motivations, faction conflict without pressure, or world rules that are not yet useful for gameplay. My role is to organize those pieces into a coherent system.

Depending on the project, I can support lore packs, story bibles, faction arcs, quest progression, branching outcomes, dialogue and character networks, tone guides, visual reference direction, keyframe notes, and moodboards for early concept development.
