About

I organize story worlds so teams can use them.

I work as a narrative designer and worldbuilding consultant for games and fictional IPs. My focus is helping developers turn early ideas into clear, structured worlds that support gameplay, character arcs, and long-term story development.

Many projects begin with strong ideas but scattered material: partial lore, disconnected mechanics, character concepts without clear motivations, or world rules that are not yet defined. My role is to organize and strengthen those elements so the narrative works as a coherent system rather than a collection of isolated pieces.

Depending on the needs of the project, I can support story bibles, faction conflicts, quest progression, tone guides, dialogue structure, character networks, visual references, and moodboards for early concept direction.
