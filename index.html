About

I turn scattered world ideas into clear development materials.

I design narrative systems and worldbuilding architecture for games, RPGs, interactive media, and long-form fictional IPs. My work helps creators organize lore, rules, factions, quests, tone, and presentation into material that collaborators can actually read, discuss, revise, and build from.

Many projects begin with strong ideas but scattered structure: partial lore, disconnected mechanics, unclear motivations, faction conflict without pressure, or world rules that are not yet useful for gameplay. My role is to find the logic underneath those pieces and turn it into a usable system.

Depending on the project, I can support lore packs, story bibles, faction arcs, quest progression, branching outcomes, dialogue and character networks, tone guides, visual reference direction, keyframe notes, and moodboards for early concept development.
