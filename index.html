About

I turn unfinished concepts into usable development material.

I work as a narrative designer and worldbuilding consultant for games and fictional IPs. My focus is helping developers turn early ideas into structured worlds that support gameplay, character arcs, faction conflict, and long-term story development.

Many projects begin with strong ideas but scattered material: partial lore, disconnected mechanics, character concepts without clear motivations, or world rules that are not yet defined. My role is to organize and strengthen those elements so the narrative works as a coherent system rather than a collection of isolated pieces.

Depending on the needs of the project, I can support story bibles, faction conflicts, quest progression, tone guides, dialogue structure, character networks, visual references, keyframe direction, and moodboards for early concept development.
